Our Story

Rooted in Faith,
Serving Huntsville

Chase Park Church of Christ has been a part of the Huntsville community for many years — a congregation built on the solid foundation of God's Word.

We are a community of ordinary people who have been changed by the extraordinary grace of Jesus Christ. We gather every week to worship God, encourage one another, and be equipped to live faithfully in the world.

Our desire is simple: to follow the New Testament pattern for the church — to be what the Bible calls us to be, no more and no less.

Our Faith

What We Believe

Our beliefs are drawn from the New Testament alone — we speak where the Bible speaks, and are silent where the Bible is silent.

The Bible as God's Word

We believe the Bible is the inspired and authoritative Word of God — sufficient and complete as our guide for faith and practice in all things.

Jesus Christ, Lord and Savior

We confess that Jesus is the Son of God, that He died for our sins, was buried, and rose again on the third day — and that He is the only way to salvation.

Baptism for the Remission of Sins

Following the New Testament pattern, we believe baptism by immersion is for the remission of sins and the gift of the Holy Spirit, for those who believe and repent.

A Cappella Worship

We worship God through singing without instrumental accompaniment, following the New Testament instruction to make melody in the heart (Eph. 5:19).

The Lord's Supper

We observe communion every Sunday in remembrance of the sacrifice of Jesus Christ, as the early church did on the first day of each week.

One Body, One Church

We believe Christ established one church — not a denomination, but a body of believers united by faith, baptism, and the Holy Spirit.

"We will speak the truth in love, growing in every way more and more like Christ, who is the head of his body, the church."

Ephesians 4:15
