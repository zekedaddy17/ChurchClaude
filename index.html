Our Community

A Place to Belong

At Chase Park, we're a family of believers following the New Testament pattern — worshipping God together, studying His Word, and caring for one another and our community.

Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or looking for a church home, you'll find open doors and warm hearts here. We'd love to see you on Sunday.

Ministries & Programs

There is a place for every member of your family at Chase Park.

Children's Ministry

Age-appropriate Bible classes and activities that plant seeds of faith in our youngest members.

Youth & Family Ministry

Building strong faith and lasting friendships for our teenagers and their families through worship, study, and fellowship.

Family Care Ministry

Caring for members during life's difficult seasons — illness, loss, and hardship — through practical love and support.

Bible Classes

In-depth Sunday and Wednesday classes for all ages — from nursery through adults — grounded in God's Word.

Home Bible Studies

Personal, flexible Bible studies in your home at no cost — a great starting point for anyone exploring the faith.

Online Resources

Live-streamed worship services, archived sermons, and video Bible studies available anytime, anywhere.
